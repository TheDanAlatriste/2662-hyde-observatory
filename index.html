Hyde Memorial Observatory

Your Window on the Universe

Come Visit Us

Open Saturdays from 7pm to 10pm

Hyde Memorial Observatory is open to public viewing, completely free of admission charge, and currently open every Saturday Night from 7 to 10pm. Fully operated by volunteers, we feature views of the night skies through our three telescopes and hold a public astronomy presentation every Saturday night.

Hyde Memorial Observatory is, as far as we know, the only observatory anywhere that is: built, furnished and operated entirely by donations, devoted exclusively to public viewing, completely free of admission charge, and staffed only by volunteers.

Located on the south side of Holmes Lake in Lincoln.
